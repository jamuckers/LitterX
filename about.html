Our Mission

What started out as a simple and fun idea has grown into a movement powered by community, and a shared sense of responsibility. We saw a problem sweeping Toronto: Litter was everywhere, and while many people cared, there was no easy, engaging way to take action in their everyday lives.

So we built a platform that combines real-world clean-up efforts with a gamified experience – empowering people to do good in a way that feels rewarding, social, and fun. Whether it's snapping a photo of litter, joining a cleanup event, or competing with friends on the leaderboard, every action contributes to something bigger.

In just a few months, our community has already removed tonnes of waste from the city, proving that when people are given the right tools and motivation, real change happens. Backed by local organizations, fueled by passionate users, and inspired by a cleaner future, we're redefining what it means to make an impact!
