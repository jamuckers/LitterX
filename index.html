How The App Works

Three simple steps to start making a difference in your community

📷

1. Report Litter

Spot litter in your area? Take a photo and upload it to the app to mark its location.

📍

2. Clean the Area

Visit the location, clean up the litter, and take an after photo to confirm completion.

🏅

3. Earn Rewards

Collect points, level up, unlock badges, and compete on leaderboards with friends.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform how communities tackle litter. By combining gamification with real-world impact, we're building a movement of engaged citizens who are passionate about keeping Toronto clean.

Every photo uploaded, every piece of litter cleaned, brings us one step closer to a cleaner, greener city. Together, we're not just cleaning streets - we're building a culture of environmental responsibility and community pride.
